# Privacy Policy

**App:** ID Helper  
**Effective date:** May 13, 2026  
**Contact:** idhelpersupport@protonmail.com

---

## Overview

ID Helper is a mobile application that helps foreigners in Poland prepare documents for residence permit applications. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

**Short version:** ID Helper does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything the app stores stays on your device.

---

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

The app stores the following data locally on your device using your phone's built-in storage (iOS SharedPreferences):

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Language preference | Display the app in your chosen language |
| Permit type(s) | Personalise your document checklist |
| Step completion states | Track your checklist progress |
| Notes you write | Store your personal reminders per step |

This data is stored **only on your device**. It is never uploaded to any server, never shared with third parties, and never transmitted over the internet.

---

## Data We Do Not Collect

- Names, email addresses, or any contact information
- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Camera, microphone, or photo library access
- Any data from the PDF documents you view or share

---

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party libraries, none of which collect personal data:

| Library | Purpose | Data collected |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Syncfusion Flutter PDF Viewer | Display PDF documents | None |
| share_plus | iOS share sheet (Save to Files, AirDrop) | None — uses the operating system's built-in sharing; we do not see what you share or where |
| path_provider | Temporary file access for sharing | None |
| Google Fonts (Inter) | Typography | None — font is bundled in the app, no network request |
| provider, shared_preferences | App state management | None |

The share function copies a PDF to a temporary file on your device and hands it to iOS. We do not receive any information about how you use the share sheet.

---

## Data Retention and Deletion

All data stored by the app lives on your device and is under your control.

- **To delete your progress and selections:** use **Settings → Reset Progress** inside the app.
- **To delete all app data:** uninstall the app from your device. iOS will remove all associated data automatically.

---

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected at all, the app complies with children's privacy regulations by design.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If the app is updated in a way that changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated and the effective date revised. Continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
